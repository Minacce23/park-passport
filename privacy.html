Park Passport Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Park Passport is built by Apricot & Ivy LLC ("we"). This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: it does not collect any.

Information we collect

None. Park Passport has no account, no login, and no sign up. We do not ask for your name, email, location, or any personal information.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter (your rides, character meets, scores, and notes) is stored only on your own device, inside the app. It is never sent to us or to anyone else. We cannot see it.

Import and export

If you import a CSV file, it is read on your device only. If you export your data, the file is saved to your device only. Nothing is uploaded.

Analytics and tracking

None. The app makes no network connections and contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Deleting your data

Because your data is only on your device, you can remove it by deleting your entries in the app or by deleting the app.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
