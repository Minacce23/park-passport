Privacy Policy Park Passport

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Park Passport is built by Acorn & Ivy LLC ("we"). This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: your log stays on your device, and the only thing ever shared is what you choose to share through the optional Friends feature.

Information we collect

By default, none. Park Passport has no required account, no login, and no sign up. We do not ask for your name, email, location, or any personal information.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter (your experiences, character meets, scores, photos, and notes) is stored only on your own device, inside the app. Your full log is never uploaded. We cannot see it.

The Friends feature (optional)

If you turn on sharing in the Friends menu, the app creates an anonymous account and stores two things on our servers (hosted on Google Firebase in the United States): the display name you choose, your headline totals (counts of experiences, unique attractions, visit days, characters met, scores logged, and coaster credits), and the name and date of your most recent experience. Anyone you give your friend code to can see that information. Nothing else is uploaded: no other individual entries, no photos, and no notes. You can turn sharing off at any time with the "Stop sharing" button, which deletes your shared profile from our servers.

Import and export

If you import a CSV file, it is read on your device only. If you export your data, the file is saved to your device only. Nothing is uploaded.

Analytics and tracking

None. The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. The only network connections it ever makes are the optional Friends syncs described above.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. The Friends feature shares only a self-chosen display name and stat totals, and is off by default.

Deleting your data

Your log lives on your device: remove it by deleting entries in the app or deleting the app. If you enabled sharing, tap "Stop sharing" in the Friends menu to delete your shared profile, or email us and we will remove it.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
