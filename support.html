Common questions

Where is my data saved?

On your device, inside the app. Nothing is sent anywhere, so your history is private to you.

How do I bring in my old trips?

Open the menu (the lines in the top corner), choose Import from CSV, and download the template. Fill it in with your experiences, character meets, and scores, then upload it back.

How do I get my data out?

Open the menu and choose Export to CSV. You will get a spreadsheet of everything you have logged.

I got a new phone. Will my data move over?

Your data lives on the device, so export to CSV on your old phone first, then import it on the new one.
